2- ICD-10-AM coding course (برنامج التدريب على الترميز الطبي)

Introduction:

  • Saudi Arabia is undergoing a major health system reform.
  • This reform will concentrate on efficiency and decreasing waste.
  • The proposed model is a model in which separation between payment and provision of services.
  • Public hospitals will be privatized and operated by a separate body.
  • Another insurance body will pay for all hospitals according to its case mix and number of patients treated.
  • Health Information Management (HIM) will be a crucial part of this reform to help to achieve the desired outcomes.
  • International Coding of Diseases (ICD-10 coding) plus Clinical Documentation Improvement (CDI) will be integral contributors to enhance HIM.

What is clinical coding?

Clinical coding is translation of the full diagnosis and associated clinical problems into numeric or alphanumeric classification by using certified clinical classification guide.

The main classification used is the International Classification of diseases developed by WHO (ICD)

Uses of clinical coding:

  • Building databases for countries, cities and hospitals.
  • Used in strategic planning in healthcare either on the national or international levels.
  • Standardization of information to compare between different hospitals or countries.
  • For proper claims management between hospitals and insurance companies.
  • Decreasing of rejection rate and denials from insurance companies.
  • Financial analysis and building proper costing system for healthcare services.
  • Research and improvement of healthcare
  • Cancer registries.
  • Measuring performance and development of KPIs.
  • Improving quality in healthcare (clinical audits and medical records reviews).
  • To comply with laws, regulations and CBAHI standards.

What modification of ICD-10 will be used in Saudi Arabia?

International Classification of Diseases- Australian modification (ICD-10-AM)

  • Classification of diseases and related clinical problems.
  • The 6th edition is approved in Saudi Arabia.
  • As WHO did not release any classification for procedures, the national center for classification in health (NCCH) in Australis has developed a classification for procedures called the Australian Classification of Health Interventions (ACHI).
  • The ICD-10-AM and ACHI system enables the translation of diagnoses and procedures and other health problems from words into an alphanumeric code, which permits easy storage, retrieval and analysis of the data.

Target audiences

(The appropriate candidates are physicians, nurses and medical records specialists with good knowledge about medical terminology) (No medical terminology classes will be provided)

For those without medical terminology knowledge, we can arrange medical terminology course first then they can proceed to clinical coding.

Prerequisites for attendees:

  • Awareness of medical terminology and human anatomy.
  • Awareness about pathophysiology of different diseases.
  • Awareness about medical and surgical procedures.
  • Awareness about different diagnostic procedures.

Resources used in coding course:

This softcopy book will make clinical coding much easier and will help in rapid education of the trainees.
